welcome to our website

The project "AgriConnect" focuses on addressing the critical issue of market access and price transparency for farmers through the development of a web application. Farmers often struggle to connect directly with potential buyers and lack information on fair market prices for their produce. AgriConnect aims to bridge this gap by providing a platform that enables farmers to access markets, connect with buyers, and obtain real-time information on market prices.
